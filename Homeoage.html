Explore the Beauty of Ozamiz City

Ozamiz City is a small city with the population slightly exceeding 130,000 people located in Misamis Occidental, Philippines. It is a center of shopping and tourism, with a few excellent world class shopping malls and hotels, as well as the points of interest like Naomi's Botanical Garden and tourists Inn, Bukagan Hill park, Fuerte de la Concepcion y del Triunfo, Immaculate Conception Pipe organ, etc. The city gets filled up with tourists in summer and autumn, during the season of traditional local festivals and celebrations.
